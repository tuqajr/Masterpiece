Required Materials for Tatreez

Start your Tatreez embroidery adventure with the right materials!

1. Cross-Stitch Fabric

Cross-stitch fabric provides a grid for precise stitching. Choose between Waste Canvas for clothing and Aida Cloth for décor or sampler projects.

  • Waste Canvas: Ideal for clothing, removable after embroidery.
  • Aida Cloth: Great for décor and sampler projects, stable grid.

2. Thread

Two popular thread options:

  • Pearl Cotton (Size 8): Smooth, durable, versatile for intricate designs.
  • Embroidery Floss (Size 25): Multiple strands for adjustable thickness.

3. Needles

Choose between Embroidery Needles (sharp tips for waste canvas) and Tapestry Needles (blunt tips for Aida Cloth).

  • Embroidery Needles: Sizes 1-5 for heavier fabrics or smaller details.
  • Tapestry Needles: Blunt, ideal for sampler or décor projects.

4. Essential Tools

Helpful tools to assist your project:

  • Embroidery Scissors
  • Tweezers
  • Pins
  • Sewing Thread
  • Embroidery Hoops (optional for beginners)
